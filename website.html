Welcome to Ancient; a site dedicated to the culture, language, religion, and history of ancient Egypt. The ancient Egyptians did not call their home “Egypt” (that name is based on the Greek pronunciation of the name of Ptah’s temple in Memphis – “Hwt-ka-Ptah”), instead they referred to it as Kemet (or Kem – the black land) or Ta Mery (the beautiful land).

Some Masterpieces

The Egyptian civilisation survived, in a relatively stable form, for a staggering three and a half thousand years.The Great Pyramid and the Sphinx are perhaps the most famous monuments associated with Ancient Egypt.
